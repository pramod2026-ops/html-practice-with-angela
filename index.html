Top movies of all time according to Pramod

My best 6 movies.

The Shawshank Redemption

Hope, friendship, and perseverance

A powerful story about Andy Dufresne, a man wrongly imprisoned who never loses hope. Inside Shawshank prison, he forms a deep friendship with Red and slowly builds a life of purpose.

This movie shows that patience and determination can overcome even the darkest situations.

Inception

A journey inside the human mind

Dom Cobb is a skilled thief who enters people's dreams to steal secrets. He is given one last mission—to plant an idea instead of stealing one.

The film blends action, science fiction, and deep emotional storytelling, making you question reality itself.

Interstellar

Love and survival beyond Earth

In a future where Earth is dying, a group of astronauts travel through a wormhole to find a new home for humanity. Cooper, a father and pilot, struggles between saving the world and staying with his daughter.

The movie beautifully mixes science with human emotion and sacrifice.

The Dark Knight

Chaos vs justice

Batman faces his greatest enemy, the Joker, who thrives on chaos and destruction. Gotham City is pushed to its limits as morality and order begin to break down.

A gripping superhero film that explores deep themes of justice, fear, and choice.

Parasite

A story of class and survival

This Korean film follows a poor family that slowly infiltrates a wealthy household by pretending to be unrelated professionals.

What starts as clever deception turns into a dark and shocking story about inequality and human nature.

Titanic

Love and tragedy at sea

A timeless romance between Jack and Rose unfolds aboard the ill-fated Titanic. Their love grows despite differences in class and society.

The movie combines emotional storytelling with one of history’s most tragic disasters.
